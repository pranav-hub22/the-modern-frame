Notable Artists

  • S. Elayaraja

    Famous for his realistic oil paintings depicting traditional Tamil women in everyday moments, blending classic South Indian aesthetics with emotional depth.

  • Bala Devi Chandrasheka

    A Bharatanatyam dancer and visual artist known for blending classical dance poses with visual storytelling in multimedia art.

  • Thota Vaikuntam

    A celebrated artist from Telangana, widely recognized for his vibrant paintings of rural South Indian life, especially depicting women in traditional attire.
